\par My research work is mainly focused on exploring the fundamental properties of massive neutrinos which have a profound impact on our understanding of particle physics, astrophysics, and cosmology. Neutrinos are omnipresent and they are the second most abundant particles in the universe after photons. There are a variety of neutrino sources (both natural and artificial) producing neutrinos over a wide range of energies in the range of $10^{-4}$ to $10^{18}$ eV or so and traveling distances from a few meters to hundreds of Gpc.
